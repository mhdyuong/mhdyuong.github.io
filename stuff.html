Buying milk tea adds up. Its like $5 a cup. Well, a hack I saw online is with Trader Joe's Black Tea Concentrate. Take some milk, add the Black Tea Concentrate and some simple brown sugar syrup ( brown sugar boiled in water). I recommend a 1 cup milk, 1/4 cup Black Tea Concentrate, and 1/3 brown sugar ratio. And letting the drink sit in the fridge for about 2 hours for the best flavor.
